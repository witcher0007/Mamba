    About Us

    The Crime Mind Analysis System (CMAS) is intended to address significant problems and challenges in the realm of criminal investigation and law enforcement.

    Integrating Diverse Data for Enhanced Predictive Accuracy

    The development of an advanced analytical system is underway, aiming to integrate diverse data types including the nature of crimes, modus operandi, and evidence for comprehensive analysis. This system seeks to enhance predictive accuracy in anticipating criminal behavior and intentions.

    Enhancing Crime Investigation

    Real-time processing capabilities are being developed to enable swift data analysis. Furthermore, emphasis is placed on creating a user-friendly interface, ensuring accessibility for law enforcement personnel with varying technical expertise. Incorporating machine learning algorithms, the system will continually adapt and refine its predictive capabilities, evolving over time to better serve the needs of crime investigation.

    Crime Statistics

    01

    Murder

    Deliberate and unlawful killing of another person with malicious intent, often resulting in severe legal penalties such as imprisonment or capital punishment.

    02

    Burglary

    The unlawful entry into a building or structure with the intent to commit a physical harm, theft or felony. It involves breaking and entering into premises without permission.

    03

    Suicide

    The act of intentionally ending one's own life. It is a tragic and complex phenomenon influenced by various factors, including mental health issues, social circumstances, and personal experiences.

    04

    Rape

    It is a heinous crime involving non-consensual sexual intercourse or penetration. It inflicts profound physical and psychological harm on the victim and is punishable by law.

    05

    Assault

    It is the intentional act of causing physical harm or injury to another person. It can involve threats or actual violence and may result in criminal charges.

    06

    Vandalism

    It is the deliberate destruction, defacement, or damage to property without the owner's consent. It includes graffiti, property destruction, and other acts that deface or harm public or private assets.

    07

    Social Media Crime

    The illegal activities committed using online platforms such as fraud, cyberbullying, harassment, identity theft, and dissemination of illegal content, exploiting the digital landscape for criminal purposes.

    08

    Drug trafficking

    It involves the illegal sale, transportation, or distribution of controlled substances such as narcotics or illicit drugs. It is a serious criminal offense punishable by law.

    09

    Human trafficking

    It is the exploitation of individuals through force, coercion, or deception for purposes such as forced labor, sexual exploitation, or organ harvesting. It is a grave violation of human rights.
